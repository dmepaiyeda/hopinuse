What is micro-transit?

Micro-transit is the future of public transportation.
Micro-transit is private transit. Our public transit is over-crowded and riddled with delays. Meanwhile for every full bus and subway train-car 3 out of 4 seats in the average car are not being used.

Carpooling

Carpool to and from transit stations and core routes. Drivers on a route can now share gas costs they would normally pay alone and enjoy their daily commute on a social level with other commuters in their carpool.

Micro-transit

Micro-transit has been expensive in the past. However by utilizing drivers that already have a similar route and matching them with commuters on that route we lower costs and make micro-transit accessible and usable for everyone.
